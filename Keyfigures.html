                Key Figures:

                Gottfried Leibniz:
                Introduced determinants in 1693.

                
                Gabriel Cramer:
                Developed Cramer's Rule in 1750.

                Hermann Grassmann: Published "Theory of Extension" in 1844,
 
                laying foundations for vector spaces.


                James Joseph Sylvester:
                Introduced the term "matrix" in 1848.


                William Rowan Hamilton:
                Discovered quaternions in 1843.

                Carl Friedrich Gauss: Developed methods for solving linear equations,

                 including Gaussian elimination.
